My Second Favorite Dish: Carrot Cake

Ingredients:

* 250 g grated carrots (about 3 medium carrots)
* 200 g all-purpose flour
* 150 g sugar
* 100 ml vegetable oil
* 3 eggs
* 1 tsp baking powder
* 1 tsp baking soda
* 1 tsp cinnamon
* 1 pinch of salt

Optional: 50 g chopped walnuts or raisins

Optional frosting: cream cheese frosting or powdered sugar

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).

2. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and sugar until smooth and creamy.

3. Add the oil and mix well.

4. In another bowl, mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.

5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture and stir until combined.

6. Fold in the grated carrots and optional nuts or raisins.

7. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.

8. Bake for 40–45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

9. Let the cake cool completely before removing it from the pan.

Optional: spread cream cheese frosting on top or sprinkle with powdered sugar.

Serving suggestion: Delicious with tea, coffee, or a glass of milk. :)
