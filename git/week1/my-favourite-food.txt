My Favorite Dish: Apple Cake

Ingredients:

* 3 medium apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
* 200 g all-purpose flour
* 150 g sugar
* 100 ml vegetable oil
* 3 eggs
* 1 tsp baking powder
* 1 tsp cinnamon
* 1 pinch of salt

Optional: powdered sugar for topping

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).

2. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and sugar until creamy.

3. Add the oil and mix well.

4. In another bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.

5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until smooth.

6. Fold in the apple slices gently.

7. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.

8. Bake for 40–45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

9. Let the cake cool for 10 minutes before removing it from the pan.

Optional: sprinkle powdered sugar on top before serving.

Serving suggestion: Serve warm or at room temperature with a cup of tea or coffee. :)
