Exploring the Danish Canon: "Danskkanon"

A cultural journey through literature, history, and everyday life in Denmark

Why I Started This Blog

My name is Paloma Cardozo, and I moved to Denmark two years ago with my family. As a foreigner, I have always been fascinated by the way Danes preserve and celebrate their culture: through stories, songs, and shared values. While studying for the Danish language exams, I discovered Danskkanon: a list of works and ideas that have shaped Danish identity.

I realized it could be much more than a list to memorize. It is a window into understanding how this country thinks, creates, and grows. Through this blog, I wish to explore each author and idea from the canon, combining cultural reflection with my love for data analysis: finding patterns in history, connecting people, values, and language.

This space is also a learning project I share with my husband and daughter. Together, we read, listen to Danish music, analyze small datasets about culture and education, and create creative ways to learn: bridging art, science, and everyday curiosity.
